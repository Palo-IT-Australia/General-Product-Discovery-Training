## What Is Heatmap Voting?

Each team member gets dot stickers (typically 3–5 dots) and silently places them on elements of any solution sketch that they find compelling, innovative, or worth exploring further. There is no discussion during voting — this ensures independent, unbiased judgement.

**Rules:**
- Vote silently and independently.
- You can spread your dots across multiple sketches or concentrate them on one.
- You can place multiple dots on the same element if you feel strongly about it.
- You may vote on your own sketch.
